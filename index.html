AWESOME FEATURES

Committed to linking the best students to the best employers

Student placement

Students from all over the country can log into our website and upload their qualifications in the varrious courses they are persuing and they will be linked to employers if their qualifications match the requrements of the employers.

When the process is done the students will be placed in varrious work stations in the country.

Monitoring work attendance

This system makes it easier to monitor the attendace of the students at work since they will be signing in through the website.

Once they have signed into the website their attendace will be sent to their varrious lecturers and also to their institutions

Monitoring productivity

The productivity of the students who are qualified for the internship programs will be monitored and the records sent to their learnig institutions.

This process will reduce the work load and time taken by their lecturers to go physically and monitor their productivity. It will also save on money that will be spent by the institutions to cater for the transport costs.

Marketing

Crust also provides a platform for companies to market themselves.

Marketing is achieved when users will log into the website and find details of a perticular company posted in the website
